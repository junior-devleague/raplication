How can you benefit from Rapplication?

When visiting this site, one may ask: "why does a site called Rapplication not incorporate any rap?" The site is not meant to teach people how to make rap music, it is meant to educate people on how to improve public speaking skills and writing skills. Furthermore, qualities needed for rap include public speaking and advanced composition. Our goal is to teach viewers how to improve these categories. We also supply outside resources for those who want to learn additional information.

Speech

You can use the speech tool to record and watch yourself speak. You will receive a score based on how fluent and well-paced your speaking was. You will be able to view your WPM (words per minute). This tool is useful because you can evaluate your eye contact, flow, and articulation.

Composition Tools

The tips and advice provided in the "Composition" section are useful if one wants to understand how to use certain literary devices, especially in poetry. Additional tips for how to write more sophisticated rhymes are also provided. This section especially focuses on metaphors, similes, and complex rhymes.
